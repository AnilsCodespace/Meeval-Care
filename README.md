# Meeval-Care
Meeval is an innovative healthcare app designed to provide invaluable support to both cancer patients and their doctors in effectively tracking and managing symptoms and medications. With its seamless user experience, Meeval offers two interfaces: a mobile app for patients and a web app for the clinic, ensuring accessibility and convenience for all users. The platform enables patients to effortlessly add or modify their medication regimens, as well as record and update their experienced symptoms. The app's intuitive design includes interactive graphs that visually represent symptom intensity, aiding patients and doctors in monitoring progress over time. Additionally, Meeval facilitates communication between patients and the clinic through patient notes, fostering a collaborative approach to care.
